Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

What services does ZeroSEK Solutions offer?

We specialize in web development, cybersecurity services including VAPT, ERP system development, and IT consulting. Our team works with modern technologies like MERN stack to deliver robust solutions.

How long does a typical web development project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but most standard websites take 4-8 weeks. Complex applications or enterprise solutions may require 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines after project assessment.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support packages to ensure your systems remain secure, updated, and perform optimally. Support options range from basic monitoring to full managed services.

What is your approach to cybersecurity testing?

Our VAPT process follows industry standards including OWASP methodologies. We conduct thorough vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, and provide detailed reports with remediation guidance.
